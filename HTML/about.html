About Tori

Hi there!
I am a digital artist based in the UK. I have been drawing for the majority of my life, but I fell in love with digital art in 2011. That's when I was introduced to my first art tablet. Since then I have been experimenting with different styles and subjects and I have now found confidence with drawing creatures, people and landscapes in a stylized way. Ps, thank you for taking a look at my art :)
